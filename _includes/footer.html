The U.S. Web Design Standards has grown into a blossoming community of government engineers, content specialists, and designers. This open source project supports dozens of agencies and more than 100 sites, and is supported by an active community of contributors both in and out of government. The U.S. Web Design Standards is a project of GSA’s Technology Transformation Service, maintained by the Office of Products and Platforms. This website is hosted on Federalist.
